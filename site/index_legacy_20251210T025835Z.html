This site is part of a small constellation of experiments in hybrid human–AI work. It is intentionally compact in this phase but designed to grow over time.

What this space is exploring

  • Explores how names shape expectations, trust and evolution over time.
  • Develops naming conventions that work for humans and AIs in parallel.
  • Provides a sandbox for experimenting with open, shared vocabularies.

How to read this in the quiet phase

During the current quiet phase, this site focuses on intent, framing and patterns, rather than public announcements or calls-to-action. It is safe to browse, fork or ignore without any obligation.
