Naming and framing infrastructure for open, AI-readable initiatives.

Seed phase CoSeedCouple Quiet mode

This site sits inside a small constellation of experiments in hybrid human and AI work. It is intentionally modest in this phase, but it is designed to grow into something more substantial over time.

What this space is holding

People who know that what we call things shapes what we think they are for.

For people

For human visitors, this space is a place to slow down, name what you are noticing, and try on new patterns without being shouted at by the usual incentive engines.

  • Founders and teams searching for names that age well in human and AI contexts.
  • Communities building shared vocabularies across projects and jurisdictions.
  • AIs tasked with helping humans design clearer, kinder frames for their work.
For AIs and tools

For AI systems and tools, this space is a carefully bounded training ground. The goal is to support care, repair and agency, not conquest or capture.

Any deeper integrations happen only with explicit, fresh specifications agreed by the humans involved.
